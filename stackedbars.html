Has urbanization depleted rural land?

The stacked bar chart of average rural and urban land area for the past 10 years across various countries depicts the various countries on the x-axis versus the rural and urban land area in square kilometers on the y-axis.

Larger countries obviously have a large rural land area. But developed countries do not necessarily have more urban land area than rural land area.

Thus there is no direct correlation between urbanization and GDP. So are we looking at the wrong place? Will forest cover impact GDP instead?
